LiveRecord is a supplementary of ActiveRecord in Rails. The main problem it tries to solve is, that every record and every property in record has its origin (who creates it and how - via what device) and its lifecycle (the changes), and these matter deserve to be traced down.

 

LiveRecord should bring any trouble in use.

 

Device

Device here takes a broad range, from IP/host of the client, to hardware device, to app (web browser or native app).
