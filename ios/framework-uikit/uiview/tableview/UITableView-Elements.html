UITableView-Elements

Since iOS 6, Header/View and Cell are treated in the same manner, thanks to introduction of UICollectionView. Even Header/FooterView is still underused for most developers, it is worthy to study it in comparison with Cell, so that you may get some insight on Cell too, needless to say on UICollectionView too.

Creating Elements

3 parts/roles in methods are introduced for creating headers and cells.

verb     Cell Header
reuseId this indicates what kind of (in term of Header/Cell subclasses) view will be applied   reuseId_Cell_for_indexpath reuseId_Header_for_section
create after reuseId is resolved, creating view is straightforward by calling dequeue* method   createCellInTableView(, indexPath)  
decorate this is a flexible part, based on varies of business   decorateCell(, atIndexPath)  

Note: verb is role in method, or role of method. Different roles of methods work together (by calling each other based on the relationship between the roles)
