2. URL

the url is on the basis of the get or post method when a networking call is made by the client

  • GET /devices/new or GET /devices/new.json
  • POST /devices or /devices.json

for example:

when iOS client want to post a device record to the server, the web site should be set as "http://0.0.0.0:3000/devices.json"

NSMutableURLRequest *request = [NSMutableURLRequest requestWithURL:[NSURL URLWithString: @"http://0.0.0.0:3000/devices.json"]];
