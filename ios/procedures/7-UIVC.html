Golden Design Rules for UIVC

  • Thin VC
  • One VC, one View, one Model
    • no subview's properties in this view

 

 

  • when addingChildViewController, do not necessarily have to addSubview. subview may be added later on demand

 

 

Golden Design Rules for UIView

 

       

不能隔代管理，应该逐级上传下达。

兄弟之间不能直接交流，要通过父亲。
